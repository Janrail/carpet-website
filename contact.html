Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

Our mobile showroom brings a comprehensive selection of carpet and flooring samples directly to your home. We'll arrange a convenient time to visit, show you various options, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote - all without you having to leave your house.

Yes, all consultations and quotes are completely free with no obligation. We believe in transparent pricing and will provide you with a detailed breakdown of all costs before any work begins.

Installation time varies depending on the room size and type of flooring. Most single rooms can be completed in a day, while larger projects may take 2-3 days. We'll provide you with a realistic timeline during your consultation.

We provide mobile flooring services throughout Greater London and surrounding areas. Contact us with your postcode to confirm we cover your area - we're often able to extend our service area for larger projects.
